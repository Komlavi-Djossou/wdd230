"What is the purpose of weather forecasts and who really finds them useful?"

The purpose of a weather forecast is to provide as accurate as possible prediction of what the weather will be like in the near future. They are important to most aspects of day to day life, including aviation, boating, other modes of transportation, farming, tourism, sports, etc. Without accurate weather forecasts people involved in activities like the ones I've listed may end up in dangerous situations they were unprepared for and end up injured or worse. Pilots need to know the weather to plan their flights, sailors need to know what the weather will be like to plan their activities, and farmers need to know what the weather will be like to help them plan watering, fertilizer and pesticide application, and harvest activities, to name a few
