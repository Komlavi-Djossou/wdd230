About Us

The purpose of the Komlavi chamber of commerce is to Support, inform and advise companies at all stages of their development. More broadly, our website contribute tremendously to the development of the economy of the land of Togo through a vast action plan.Hee we observe and analyze the needs of businesses and regions, produce information and economic opinions, we also help build the best possible environment for business development and support them in their search for competitiveness. If you are entrepreneurs who have been running successfully or unsuccessfully your companies in Togo for years and for a while, please you can joins us. We help also new companies that are emerging, and especially have difficulties to find the support to stand firm. For foreign investors who are willing to implement their business in Lomé but don’t know how to go, we assist them to do that.
